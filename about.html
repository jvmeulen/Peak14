About Tony

Tony Anderson is the Principal of Peak14 Consulting — an independent consultancy serving the heavy construction industry across the United States and Canada. With more than a decade of hands-on experience with HCSS software and a deep background in construction operations, Tony brings a perspective that goes far beyond the average trainer.

Tony's work with HCSS began as a user — learning HeavyBid and HeavyJob from the estimating desk and the field, understanding firsthand where the software delivers and where teams struggle to make it stick. That experience became the foundation for Peak14: practical training, grounded in how construction actually works, delivered by someone who's been there.

Beyond software training, Tony works with construction executives on the strategic and operational challenges that shape a company's long-term health. Long-term planning, process efficiency, organizational design, and building the leadership culture that keeps great people — these are the engagements Tony brings the same direct, no-nonsense approach to that has defined his HCSS work.

Every Peak14 engagement is delivered personally by Tony. No junior staff, no subcontractors — just a principal consultant fully invested in your team's success.
