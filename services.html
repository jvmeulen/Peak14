Field Management Cost Tracking Reporting

HeavyJob connects the field to the office — but only when your whole team knows how to use it. Peak14 trains foremen, project managers, and office staff together, so the data actually flows.

Tony's HeavyJob training is built around how construction actually works. Foremen get practical, no-nonsense instruction they can apply the next morning. Project managers learn to read reports and act on cost data in real time. Office staff learn setup, coding, and keeping the system clean.

What's Covered

  • Daily diary entry, crew management, and time allocation
  • Equipment and material tracking on the job
  • Budget vs. actual cost analysis and reporting
  • Project setup, cost codes, and WBS structure

  • Subcontractor management and quantity tracking
