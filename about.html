About Tony

Tony Anderson is the Principal of Peak14 Consulting, bringing deep expertise in HCSS software and a firsthand understanding of the construction industry. With years of experience helping contractors get the most out of their estimating and field management tools, Tony delivers training that's practical, efficient, and built around how real construction teams work.

Peak14 specializes exclusively in HCSS products — HeavyBid for estimating and HeavyJob for field cost tracking. This focused expertise means clients get training that goes far beyond the manual, tailored to their specific workflows, crew structures, and project types.

Whether working with a small contractor just getting started with HeavyBid or helping a large civil company standardize HeavyJob across multiple divisions, Tony's approach is always hands-on, direct, and results-focused.
